GLP Me Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

GLP Me ("GLP Me," "we," "us," or "our") is a personal wellness tracker for medication and pill routines, doses, symptoms, nutrition, hydration, weight, activity, and progress. This policy explains how the app handles information.

GLP Me is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication dosing, medication adjustment, or titration recommendations.

App Data And Private iCloud Sync

Your medication or pill routines, dose history, injection sites, symptom check-ins, weight history, nutrition and hydration logs, imported Health values, progress photos, notes, preferences, and reminders are stored by the app. GLP Me does not require an account and does not upload this information to a GLP Me-operated server.

When iCloud is available, Apple may privately synchronize app data through your personal iCloud account so it is available on your Apple devices. This data is handled under your iCloud account and Apple's terms and privacy practices. GLP Me's operator cannot browse your private iCloud database. You can manage iCloud in iOS Settings.

Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, GLP Me requests read-only access only to the categories needed for features you select: body weight, steps, active energy, workouts, dietary calories, dietary protein, and dietary water. Apple Health access occurs only after you grant permission. Imported values are stored with your private app data and are not sent to a GLP Me-operated server. You can change Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings.

Voice Food Input

If you choose voice food input, GLP Me requests microphone and speech-recognition permission and requires Apple's on-device speech recognition. GLP Me uses the resulting transcript as a food-search term. GLP Me does not save the audio recording or send it to a GLP Me-operated server.

Food Search And Barcode Lookup

When you use food search or barcode lookup, the search term or barcode is sent over HTTPS directly to Open Food Facts so the app can return matching packaged-food information. These requests are used only to provide the search result. GLP Me does not attach an account, advertising identifier, medication history, symptom history, Apple Health data, or progress photos to these requests.

Notifications And Photos

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally through iOS. Progress photos are selected through Apple's system photo picker and are stored with your private app data, including private iCloud sync when available. We do not receive your notification schedule or photos.

Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

GLP Me does not include third-party advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs. We do not sell personal information, use health information for advertising, or track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

Retention And Deletion

App information remains until you delete it in GLP Me or remove it through your device and iCloud controls. Deletions made in GLP Me are synchronized to the private iCloud store when sync is available. Open Food Facts may retain request information under its operational and privacy practices.

Your Choices

  • You can use GLP Me without connecting Apple Health.
  • You can enter foods manually instead of using online search or barcode lookup.
  • You can manage Health, microphone, speech recognition, camera, photo, notification, and iCloud permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can remove individual entries or use Delete All Data in the app. You can also manage the app's iCloud data through Apple's system controls.

Children's Privacy

GLP Me is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the food-search service, contact us so we can investigate and delete it where applicable.

Changes

We may update this policy as GLP Me changes. The effective date above identifies the latest version.
