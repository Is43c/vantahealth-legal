GLP Me Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

GLP Me ("GLP Me," "we," "us," or "our") is a personal wellness tracker for medication schedules, injections, symptoms, nutrition, hydration, weight, and progress. This policy explains how the app handles information.

GLP Me is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication dosing, medication adjustment, or titration recommendations.

Information Stored On Your Device

Your medication schedule, injection history, dose, injection site, symptom check-ins, weight history, nutrition logs, hydration logs, progress photos, notes, preferences, and reminders are stored locally on your device. GLP Me does not require an account and does not upload this information to our servers.

Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, GLP Me requests read-only access to your latest body-weight sample. Apple Health access occurs only after you grant permission. Imported weight data is stored locally in GLP Me and is not sent to our servers. You can change Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings.

Food Search And Barcode Lookup

When you use food search or barcode lookup, the search term or barcode is sent over HTTPS to our lookup service and its nutrition-data provider so the app can return matching food information. These requests are used only to provide the search result. GLP Me does not attach an account, advertising identifier, medication history, symptom history, Apple Health data, or progress photos to these requests.

Notifications And Photos

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally through iOS. Progress photos are selected through Apple's system photo picker and remain in the app's local storage. We do not receive your notification schedule or photos.

Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

GLP Me does not include third-party advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs. We do not sell personal information, use health information for advertising, or track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

Retention And Deletion

Local information remains on your device until you delete it in GLP Me, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app. Food-search and barcode requests may be retained temporarily in service-provider security logs under their operational retention practices.

Your Choices

  • You can use GLP Me without connecting Apple Health.
  • You can enter foods manually instead of using online search or barcode lookup.
  • You can manage Health, camera, photo, and notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can remove local entries in the app or delete the app to remove its local data.

Children's Privacy

GLP Me is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the food-search service, contact us so we can investigate and delete it where applicable.

Changes

We may update this policy as GLP Me changes. The effective date above identifies the latest version.
