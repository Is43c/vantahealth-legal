VantaHealth Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

VantaHealth ("VantaHealth," "we," "us," or "our") provides wellness-focused food logging, hydration, training, sleep, medication-context reminders, and body-composition coaching. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

VantaHealth is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication dosing, medication adjustment, or titration recommendations.

Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address, authentication identifier, and sign-in provider information through Supabase Auth.

Health, Fitness, And Wellness Information

You may enter or connect information such as age, sex, height, weight, body-fat estimates, food logs, macro and micronutrient estimates, hydration logs, workout logs, sleep data, step data, GLP-1/TRT context, reminder schedules, appetite, and digestive-comfort notes.

Apple Health data is read only after you grant permission. You can manage Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings.

Food Search And Optional Integrations

When configured, food searches may be sent to food-data providers such as Nutritionix, USDA FoodData Central, or Open Food Facts to return nutrition results. Optional photo or body-analysis endpoints are only used if configured and enabled.

Local Diagnostics

VantaHealth stores limited diagnostics on device, such as local food-search and logging funnel events, to help debug the beta experience. We do not currently use third-party analytics SDKs for behavioral tracking.

Purchase Information

If you start a trial or subscription, Apple processes payment details. VantaHealth uses RevenueCat to receive purchase status, entitlement status, product identifiers, transaction metadata, and the app user identifier used to grant Premium access.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide food logging, nutrition targets, hydration targets, training and sleep context, medication reminders, and wellness coaching.
  • Sync your VantaHealth state across devices when you sign in.
  • Grant, restore, and manage Premium access for active trials and subscriptions.
  • Export or delete your data when requested.
  • Maintain app safety, security, and reliability.

We do not sell personal information or health data. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Cloud Sync

Signed-out use is local to your device. If you sign in, your VantaHealth app state is synced to Supabase in a per-user row protected by Supabase Row Level Security. Supabase acts as a service provider/processor for authentication, database storage, and secure transport.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or clear it from Settings.

Cloud sync data remains until you delete your account or we no longer need it to provide the service. In the app, go to More -> Account & Sync or More -> Privacy to delete your account and associated cloud sync row. Deletion also clears local VantaHealth data on that device.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Use VantaHealth signed out.
  • Export local VantaHealth data as JSON from More -> Privacy.
  • Clear local food logs or all local VantaHealth data from More -> Privacy.
  • Delete your account from More -> Account & Sync or More -> Privacy.
  • Manage Apple Health, camera, and photo permissions in iOS Settings.

Security

We use HTTPS/TLS for network transport. Supabase Auth manages account sessions, and cloud sync rows are scoped by authenticated user ID. No security system is perfect, and you should not use VantaHealth for emergency or clinical decisions.

Children's Privacy

VantaHealth is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The effective date above will show the latest version.
